General medical datasets#

OpenNeuro#

OpenNeuro: A free and open platform for sharing MRI, MEG, EEG, iEEG, ECoG, and ASL data.

The Cancer Imaging Archive#

The Cancer Imaging Archive (TCIA): is a service which de-identifies and hosts a large archive of medical images of cancer accessible for public download. You can also Submit Your Data, Many TCIA datasets are submitted by the user community.

US Governemnet Data#

US gov data: The home of the U.S. Government’s open data, you will find data, tools, and resources to conduct research, develop web and mobile applications, design data visualizations.

IEEE dataport#

IEEE dataport: Easily store and access hundreds of datasets, including big data datasets, through IEEE's dataset storage and dataset search platform, DataPort.

University of California Irvine dataset repository#

University of California Irvine (UCI): UCI Machine Learning Repository.

PhysioNet#

PhysioNet: is a repository of freely-available medical research data, managed by the MIT Laboratory for Computational Physiology.

EU Open Data Portal#

The EU Open Data Portal: is the point of access to public data published by the EU institutions, agencies and other bodies. Information can be used and reused for commercial or non-commercial purposes. The portal is a key instrument of the EU open data strategy.

Cambridge Centre for Ageing and Neuroscience (Cam-CAN)#

Cambridge Centre for Ageing and Neuroscience (Cam-CAM) data repository for (Cam-CAN) dataset can be found here Cam-CAN archive.

Health data#

health data: dedicated to making high value health data more accessible to entrepreneurs, researchers, and policy makers in the hopes of better health outcomes for all.
